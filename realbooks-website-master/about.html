Meet Our Founders

Neeraj Kumar Jain

Co Founder & CIO

Neeraj is a well known Chartered Accountant and the brain behind the RealBooks platform. He also has top notch experience with brands like PwC , though as he would be the first to admit, he’s less of a CA and more a tech junkie! He is constantly trying to come out with ways and means of automating processes and making accounting more of a science and less of an art. An avid Pranic Healer, he frequently travels to take classes both in India and abroad.

Anurag Mohta

Co Founder & CEO

Anurag completed his MBA from IIM Lucknow, and has several years of experience in investment banking and derivatives trading at HSBC and Deutsche Bank, across London and Mumbai. His determination has ensured RealBooks is today among the leading global players in its domain. He’s also a passionate golfer, so if you don’t find him in the boardroom, he is probably working on his swing on the golf course!
