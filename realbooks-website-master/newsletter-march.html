March, 2021

In the last edition of this Financial Year, we look back with a lot of gratitude towards our team and clients for the support shown by them. RealBooks was adopted by numerous businesses who truly understood the value of being online and accessible, having data and documents on the cloud and getting more and more control in a distributed environment.

One major area of focus for RealBooks has been the auto dealership sector and in this year, we added dealerships across Maruti, Hyundai, Mahindra, Ford, Skoda, Ashok Leyland, Kia, Hero and more.

The ability for clients to segregate their accounts for individual showrooms and workshops and consolidate them on a real-time basis plus be able to handle very large volumes of data and multiple GSTINs was something that most dealerships were struggling with.

We look forward to the new financial year with renewed vigour. We hope everyone stays safe and gets vaccinated at their due turn.

Till the next Financial Year...... signing off!
