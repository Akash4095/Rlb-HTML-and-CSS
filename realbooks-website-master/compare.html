We were earlier using Tally for most of our clients, which involved long hours of commuting by our team, spending time in client offices and over all inefficiency. Since 2016, we have been using RealBooks for over 20 of our clients. The document scanning and attachment feature of RealBooks is world class – clients simply scan documents, and our team can do the entry sitting in our offices. The attached documents, approval process and notifications all have enabled us to manage our clients better.
