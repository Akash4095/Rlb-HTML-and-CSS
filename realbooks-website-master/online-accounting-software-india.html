Here's how RealBooks keeps your business GST compliant
GST in transactions

Now start getting all your taxes in place with RealBooks. This online accounting software can now track all your relevant details in the invoices and bills so that your transactions are in compliance with the government mandates. Other details like GST levied on goods and services along with the place of supply can also be mentioned through this cloud accounting software.

e-Way bills for consignments

RealBooks is an all-inclusive online accounting software where you can create e-Way bills directly. RealBooks can detect all your transactions that have an e-Way bill and can track the bill information as well. The cloud accounting software can capture this information and mention them in your transport copies and invoices for ease of access.

On-time returns filing

You can file your GST returns directly from RealBooks. This online accounting software can automatically calculate all your GST liability and also assist in sending the transactions to the GST portal. You can also file your tax returns directly from this cloud accounting software.

GST treatment for contacts and items

With an online accounting software India like RealBooks, you can associate HSN/SAC codes with all your goods and services. This way, you can record the GSTIN at the time of creation. The next time you start a transaction, the corresponding information can be auto-filled.
